Standards

Our New Constitution ("Standards") be our charter to happiness.

Article 1: Executive

Our head of state leads our people, SLFT and the SLWF.

  • Faith: Leader of Buddhists to set "Better Way".
  • Law: Legal immunity to murder; may pardon any.
  • Policy: Selects unelected PM and cabinet freely.
  • Defense: Directs armed forces or police.
  • Business: Issues directives for all industries.
  • Overrides: Overrides parliament and judiciary.

Article 2: Parliament

Our representative democracy honors people's requests.

  • Voting: Internet voting, instruct in advance.
  • MPs: Auction vote buy 500 MPs + 100 staffers.
  • Senators: MPs elect 50 senators to audit laws.
  • Lords: Senators pick 5 lords to guide executive.
  • Finance: Federal tax citizens for exec budget.

Article 3: Courts

Our courts ensures the rule of law for justice.

  • Responsibility: Exec's photo hung to personify justice.
  • Court Officers: Justices sourced online on performance.
  • Online Courts: Cases done online on crowd signal.
  • Conclusions: Public can petition; weighted-poll decisions.
  • Overruling: Gov't selects, interpets and overrides judicial decisions.

Article 4: People

We citizens swear allegiance to our own people as our sovereign.

  • Genetics: Citizenship by blood or agreement under SLFT.
  • SLFT Citizens: First-class hereditary citizens with priority benefits.
  • NIC Citizens: Second-class citizens and PRs with fewer benefits.
  • Foreigners: May be given citizenship only by executive approval.
  • Values: Our people believe in service, excellence and integrity.

Article 5: Rights

We have freedoms, rights, privileges, duty and obligations.

  • Speech: Freedom to free thought, free speech and transparency.
  • Identity: Equal rights to identity, protected characteristics and privacy.
  • Income: Birth privilege to earn through our systemized SLWF.
  • Life: Duty to health, nutrition, safety, dignity and happiness.
  • Solutions: Obligation to problem solve, think critical and lead teams.

Article 6: Wealth

We work to earn our fortune as united investors.

  • Management: Sri Lanka Wealth Fund oversee prosperity.
  • Distribution: Wealth won fairly to lower inequalities.
  • MNCs: Award 30% of profit to strategic partners.
  • SLWF: Executive grants monopoly charters to anything.
  • Investors: All citizens are investors under SLWF.

Article 7: Military

All of us are ready to fight for our freedom when we must.

  • Factor: Proactive defense of sovereignty.
  • Control: Exec is commander-in-chief, gov't heads are officers.
  • Service: Mandatory service for teens on merit.
  • Armory: Citizens own defense technology to conquer enemies.
  • Reserve: Citizens are reserved into militias.

Article 8: Property

All this is our people's land to own, enjoy and protect.

  • Territory: 65,610 sq km and seas are SLFT property to develop.
  • Ownership: Citizens own property, safeguarded by law, especially homes.
  • Distribution: Equitably compete for resources; no monopolies.
  • Sustainability: Sensible activities to protect healthy environment.
  • Assets: We pledge to raise worth of our estates.

Article 9: Ethics

All of our good-will will lead to our salvation.

  • Policy State policy is globalist capitalist.
  • Direction: Responsible departmental business dealings.
  • Trade: Exec decides healthy international investing.
  • Youth: We support our own SMEs in dealings early.
  • Media: Exec directs any report literacy directions.

Article 10: Future

My G-d has given us our glory with his own hands.

  • Succession: People appoints any as successor with vote.
  • Amendments: People by proxy update laws.
  • Time: People evolve executive as beneficial.
  • Treasury: People votes to use reserves without veto.
  • Providence: May G-d's blessings continue guiding our acts.
